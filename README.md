# Qurbani Hat
## Qurbani Hat is a digital animal hat for upcoming Eid-Ul-Azha. Here we are providing the country best animals direct from farmer and farm who are mantain proper health and hygines. And we ensure our hat animals are consume 100% pure and organic feed and they are consume green grass.

## Key features of Qrrbani Hat
1. Here user can easily choose here desire animal for qurbani.
2. We are ensure 100% anti-biotic and hormane free animal
3. Our animals are grown totally organic way and healthy environment.
4. We are provide reasonable price your desire animals.
5. Here is not middle man or broker for buy your qurbani from us.

2. React Icons
3. DaisyUI
